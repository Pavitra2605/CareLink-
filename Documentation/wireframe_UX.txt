# CARELINK – Low-Fidelity Wireframes & Figma AI Prompts

## 1. Purpose of This Document

This document provides **low-fidelity wireframe sketches** (ASCII-style) and **ready-to-use Figma AI prompts** for each core screen of CARELINK. It bridges UX planning and visual design, enabling rapid prototyping with clarity and consistency.

## 9. Notes for Designers

* Keep layouts minimal and linear
* One primary action per screen
* Large touch targets (minimum 48px)
* Text-first, icon-supported UI
* Emergency actions always visible

---

## 10. Conclusion

These low-fidelity wireframes and Figma AI prompts provide a clear foundation for rapid UI prototyping. They ensure CARELINK’s design remains simple, safe, and accessible while enabling quick transition from concept to interactive mockups.
